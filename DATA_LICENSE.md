## Sample data

The files under `examples/sample-data/` are synthetic fixtures created only for parser, artifact, report, and static-site validation. They are not broker data, exchange data, or investment research.

Synthetic sample fixtures and generated sample artifacts under `examples/sample-artifacts/` may be reused for testing, documentation, and demo purposes under the same Apache-2.0 project license unless a file states otherwise.

## Third-party market data

Do not commit broker exports, paid vendor data, TradingView exports, futures data, economic calendar dumps, or any other third-party market data unless redistribution rights are verified and documented.

Acceptable public-repo data policy:

- synthetic fixtures: allowed;
- user-owned local exports: allowed only in private/local workspaces;
- third-party data with unclear redistribution rights: forbidden;
- third-party data with explicit redistribution permission: allowed only with source, license, and date documented.

## User responsibility

Users are responsible for validating the rights, accuracy, freshness, and fitness of any data they load into this package.
